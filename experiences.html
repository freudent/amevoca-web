Written testimonials

"My family finally understands me."

For years I felt like people were taking my words the wrong way. I'd say something warm and they'd think I was cold. I'd offer help and they'd think I was being perfunctory. Using Amevoca was like someone finally turned up the volume on my emotional content. My care comes through now.

— Early user, living with Home for 18 months
"I can hear myself now."

I didn't realize how much I was guessing about my own intonation until Amevoca made it audible. Now I can actually hear what I'm producing. I can adjust it. It's like suddenly having a mirror for your voice. Everything feels more intentional.

— Musician and early tester, using Go daily
"Work became easier."

In meetings, people seemed to consistently misunderstand my tone. I'd offer an idea and they'd react like I was being critical. With Amevoca, that stopped. People actually engage with what I'm saying instead of interpreting around it. It's made my job feel less exhausting.

— Professional, team lead, using Home for 20 months
"Small talk works now."

I used to dread casual conversation. I could never figure out if people thought I actually cared about what we were talking about. They seemed distant even when I was trying to be warm. Amevoca changed that. They feel the warmth now because they can hear it. It's still them responding to me — but now they're responding to the real thing.

— Long-term friend and user, two years with Home
"I can finally sound like myself."

For years I was the loud person. Enthusiastic, but too loud. I'd interrupt people, thinking I was joining in, and they'd get annoyed. I never understood why. I felt warm and caring — but apparently I sounded aggressive and rude. Then I discovered I could sing beautifully. My voice in singing sounded like the person I actually was. I realized: the difference was my chest voice had rich harmonics in singing that my speaking voice didn't have. With Amevoca, my speaking voice finally carries that clarity. Now people hear what I've always felt — calm, thoughtful, caring. I sound like the person my close friends have always known me to be.

— Student and early user, speaking with Amevoca daily
