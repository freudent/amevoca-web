04 — privacy

We collect what's needed. Nothing more.

We're a small independent company. We don't want your data any more than you want us to have it.

01

The website

We collect your name, email address, and shipping information to process your order and get your unit to you. That's it. We don't run ad trackers. We don't sell or share your information.

02

The device

Resonero processes sound acoustically in real time. It does not store audio. It does not transmit audio. It is not network-connected — there is no Wi-Fi, no Bluetooth, no cloud component of any kind. The sound in your room stays in your room.

03

The listening test

Anonymous. We generate a reference number locally — we have no way to connect it to you unless you choose to share it. No account required.

No network connection isn't a constraint we work around. It's a design choice. A device in your home that processes conversation has no business on the internet.

05 — quick answers
Can I return it?

Yes. If Resonero isn't working for you, contact info@resonero.com within 30 days of delivery. We'll work it out. We read every email.

Is the deposit refundable if I change my mind?

Yes, until your parts are ordered (typically end of week 1). After that, parts are in transit and non-returnable to suppliers. Email us immediately if you need to cancel.

When exactly will balance payment be requested?

When your unit ships — around week 3.5–4. We'll email you with the final amount and a payment link before it leaves.

Does it come with any warranty?

Yes. We stand behind what we build. Contact info@resonero.com for any hardware issue within the first year.
