02 — the physics

It's not a character flaw. It's physics.

Your voice carries a melody — intonation — that signals what you're actually feeling underneath the words. The warmth. The irony. The care. But that melody only survives at distances closer than social norms allow.

Across a dinner table, in a meeting room, in a classroom — intonation melody is physically absent from the signal that reaches other people. Listeners' brains reconstruct it automatically from harmonics. For many people, that reconstruction is reliable. For a significant number, it isn't — and the misreading feels exactly like direct perception.

The same problem runs in both directions. As a speaker, without accurate intonation information, producing the tone you intend is like tuning an instrument through a funhouse mirror.

03 — the solution

Resonero delivers what conversation has always been missing.

A small device in your room. No interface to manage. No mode to enter. No explanation to offer. The emotional layer of speech arrives — clearly, continuously — in the one context where it has always been stripped out: face-to-face speech at social distances.

when listening

Intonation melody arrives clearly — not reconstructed, not inferred. The warmth, nuance, and intent in someone's voice become legible without effort. You stop second-guessing what people mean and start simply hearing it.

when speaking

With accurate intonation information in the room, you perceive what you're actually producing — and can shape it toward what you intend, naturally, without conscious effort. Conversation stops feeling like work.

Like eyeglasses — the augmentation disappears into the experience it enables. Most augmented reality systems announce themselves. Resonero doesn't. You don't change. The room does.

07 — common questions

A few things people ask first.

What does Resonero actually do?

Resonero is a room acoustics device implementing Intonation Augmentation (IA). It captures vocal signal before intonation melody dissipates at social distances and delivers it back into the room acoustically — so both speaker and listener receive clear intonation melody. No app. No interface. Simply present.

Will it help me specifically?

That depends on whether unreliable intonation melody reconstruction is part of what you're experiencing. The listening test gives you a reliable self-assessment before you commit to anything. We'd rather you know.

Is this a medical device or therapy?

No. Resonero is a wellness tool that supports effective communication. It doesn't replace professional care and we don't position it as a treatment for any condition. If you're working with a clinician, explore Resonero in conversation with them.

Do other people in the room notice it?

Almost nothing to see. Sonic and visual invisibility are intentional design features. Early users describe conversations where no one noticed the device — only that the conversation felt different.
