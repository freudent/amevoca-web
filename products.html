Both models contain identical processor technology. The difference is form factor and power delivery. Choose based on your life, not the hardware specs.

The processing is the same

Whether you choose Home or Go, the processor analyzes incoming audio in real-time, extracts the intonation pitch that physics removed, and delivers it back into the room through the speaker. The acoustic result is identical: both speaker and listener finally have the emotional content of speech, clearly and directly.

The choice is purely about context and lifestyle.
