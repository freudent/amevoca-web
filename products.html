Home and Go.

Two versions. Same Intonation Augmentation technology. Different form factors for different spaces.

01 — resonero home

Install it and forget about it.

Resonero Home is the permanent installation. Plug it into the wall, place the bookshelf speaker where the conversation happens, and leave it. Living rooms, home offices, conference rooms, therapy practices — any space where people consistently gather to talk. The 3m cable gives you flexibility in placement. Nobody adjusts it. Nobody notices it. It just works.

The bookshelf speaker is sized to disappear into a room acoustically. IA arrives as part of the ambient sound, not as an identifiable separate source. Several users have described the result as something like magic — a room that simply feels different, without being able to say why. We think we know why. The emotional layer of conversation, reliably absent at social distances, is present. That turns out to be noticeable.

02 — resonero go

Take it with you.

Resonero Go is the portable unit. The processor clips to your bag strap. Speaker and power bank ride inside the bag. At your destination — a meeting, a classroom, a client visit — set the speaker on the table and plug in the power bank. That's the current setup: two steps, under a minute. The bag clip, now in development, will eliminate even that: processor stays clipped to the strap, speaker and battery stay in the bag — working continuously. In the hallway. When you sit down for class or a meeting. When you get up again. No setup, no transition, no interruption.

04 — how many?

One per room. Natural progression.

Resonero works in the room it's in. Most users start with one unit in the space where they spend the most conversational time — typically the living room or a home office. Additional units extend the benefit to other spaces.

There's no system to configure between units. Each one is independent.
