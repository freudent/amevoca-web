How it works

Week 1: You order and make your deposit (or pay in full). Production is immediately funded.
Weeks 2-3: We source parts, begin assembly. Your unit is built that week.
Week 3-4: Parts arrive. Final assembly happens. We prepare your shipment.
Week 4: Your Amevoca ships. If split payment, your final invoice arrives.

Why this model?

Pre-orders fund production directly. We don't build inventory. We don't warehouse products. You fund the production of your unit, and we deliver it within 4 weeks.

This keeps costs low, ensures quality control, and means no one is speculating on inventory levels. You get a device built specifically because you ordered it.

You fund production. We stay independent.

Current capacity

We're accepting pre-orders for our first production run: up to 25 units (Home + Go combined).

Once we reach 25 orders, the pre-order will close. If you'd like to be notified when the next batch becomes available, we have a waitlist.

Sold out?

If the current batch reaches capacity, you can join the waitlist. We'll notify you as soon as the next production run opens for pre-order (typically 4-6 weeks after the previous batch ships).
