Frequently asked questions

What is intonation pitch?

The lowest, most fundamental frequency in your voice — the note you're singing, the emotional coloring of your speech. It's the component that carries emotional content. In normal speech, it's also the component that dissipates so quickly that it's absent at social distances.

Will Amevoca work for me?

If you've noticed your voice doesn't come across as you intend, or if you watch videos of yourself and don't recognize your emotional content, Amevoca addresses exactly this. If you're curious about your own intonation perception, take the listening test. It will give you a concrete sense of what we're addressing.

How long does it take to see results?

Most people notice differences immediately — the room sounds warmer, conversations feel different. Deeper changes (learning to modulate your own intonation, others consistently understanding you better) typically emerge over weeks as you use it regularly.

Home or Go — which should I get?

Home if you spend most of your time in one space (office, home, studio). Go if you move between locations (coffee shops, offices, travel). Both contain identical processing. The choice is about lifestyle, not features.

Can I use it with a headset or external speakers?

The system is designed as a complete unit — processor + speaker delivering the augmented signal back into the room. Headsets would require a different design. External speakers compatible with standard 3.5mm audio output are possible with a simple adapter (Home only).

Is it always listening?

The processor analyzes room audio continuously when powered on. It's always processing, always delivering intonation pitch. There's no mode to toggle, no on/off switch for the processing itself — it's seamless, like a always-on hearing aid.

What about privacy?

The processor doesn't record, store, or transmit audio. It analyzes sound in real-time, extracts the intonation pitch component, and outputs it locally. Nothing leaves your device.

How do I set it up?

Unbox, plug in the power, connect the speaker (if separate), place in your room. That's it. No app, no configuration, no wireless pairing. It works immediately.
