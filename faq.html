Frequently asked questions.

If your question isn't here, email info@resonero.com. We read everything and reply within 48 hours.

01 — how it works
What does Resonero actually do?

Resonero is a room acoustics device implementing Intonation Augmentation (IA). It captures vocal signal before intonation melody dissipates at social distances, and delivers it back into the room acoustically — so both speakers and listeners receive clear intonation melody throughout conversation. There is no app, no interface, no mode to enter. It is simply present.

How does it deliver intonation melody?

A processor unit captures near-field vocal signal where the fundamental pitch (F0) is present. It processes this to isolate and amplify intonation melody, then delivers it into the room via a speaker positioned at conversational distance. The result is ambient — all participants receive it simultaneously. No headphones. No personal device required.

Do other people in the room notice it?

Almost nothing to see. Visual and sonic invisibility are intentional design features. Early users describe conversations where no one mentioned the device — they just noticed the conversation felt different. The speaker is small and sits on a shelf or desk.

Does it work with headphones or earbuds?

No — and deliberately so. Resonero is a room acoustics approach. Headphones change the dynamic of conversation. Resonero works with the room, not against it.

Is there any setup involved?

Minimal. Connect processor to speaker via the included cable, power via USB, and place the speaker within conversational range. No pairing, no app, no calibration sequence. The welcome card from Eric walks you through it.

02 — will it help me
How do I know if this is for me?

Take the listening test. It's a short psychoacoustic assessment that measures how reliably your auditory cortex reconstructs intonation melody from harmonic information. Results are immediate and anonymous. We built the test specifically so you don't have to guess.

Is this a medical device or therapy?

No. Resonero is a wellness tool that supports effective communication. It doesn't replace professional care and is not positioned as a treatment for any condition. If you're working with a clinician or therapist, we welcome you to explore Resonero in conversation with them — not instead of them.

Can I use it alongside speech therapy or communication coaching?

Yes — they address different things and can be complementary. Speech therapy works on production strategies and technique. Resonero addresses the signal itself. Bring it into the conversation with your therapist.

What about Forbrain and similar approaches?

Forbrain and polyvagal-based approaches target nervous system stress response directly, via bone conduction. Resonero targets intonation melody identification — a different mechanism, likely addressing a different population. If one hasn't worked for you, the other may address something different entirely. 03 — products and ordering
What's the difference between Home and Go?

Same Intonation Augmentation technology. Home is the permanent installation — bookshelf speaker, 3m cable, USB wall power. Put it in the room where conversation happens and leave it. Go is the portable unit — the processor clips to your bag strap, speaker and power bank ride inside the bag. At your destination, unpack and it's working. Pack up when you leave. See products for a full comparison.

What's the locker issue with Resonero Go?

Resonero Go is sensitive to closed, hard-surfaced spaces — specifically lockers. The acoustics in an enclosed hard-walled space can cause feedback. Mute the unit before placing it in a locker. We tell people this upfront because we'd rather you know than discover it.

Can I use it at work without explaining it to colleagues?

Yes. The device is small and unobtrusive. You are not required to explain it to anyone. Most early users don't. The people around them notice conversations feel different; they don't attribute it to a device.

How many units do I need?

One per room. Resonero works in the space it's in. Most users start with the room where they spend the most conversational time. Additional units extend the benefit to other rooms. Each unit is independent — no system to configure between them.

05 — privacy
Is the device listening to me?

Resonero processes sound in real time to deliver intonation melody acoustically. It does not record audio. It does not store audio. It does not transmit audio. There is nothing to store or send — the processing happens entirely within the device itself, in the moment.

Is Resonero connected to the internet?

No. There is no Wi-Fi, no Bluetooth, no network component of any kind. The device has no way to transmit anything. A device in your home that processes conversation has no business on the internet, and ours isn't.

What information does the website collect?

We collect your name, email, and shipping address to process your order and deliver your unit. We don't run ad trackers. We don't sell or share your information with third parties.

How do I know if Resonero will help me specifically?

Visit the illusion page — Eric's demonstration video of the Missing Fundamental Illusion lets you hear directly whether your auditory pathway reconstructs H1 reliably. That's the most honest self-assessment available before you buy. We'd rather you know.
